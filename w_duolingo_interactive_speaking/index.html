一、个人经历类（基于纽约徒步经历）

话题1：难忘的经历

Questions

  1. What is the most unforgettable outdoor experience you have ever had?
  2. Who did you share this unforgettable experience with, and why was it so special to you?
  3. How did this experience change your attitude towards outdoor activities or daily life?

Answers

  1. My unforgettable experience is hiking in New York with my brother. The summer snow mountains were beautiful. I felt excited when I reached the top.
  2. I shared it with my brother. I love nature and wanted to take photos. Hiking helped me forget work stress and relax my mind.
  3. It made me love outdoor activities more. I became braver and more confident. I also learned to slow down my busy life.

话题2：兴趣爱好

  1. What is your favorite hobby and how did you develop this interest?
  2. How often do you engage in this hobby, and what do you usually do during the process?

  2. I hike when I have time, like weekends. I often go to mountains with my brother to enjoy nature and take photos.
  3. Hiking keeps me healthy. It helps me forget work stress and makes me feel relaxed and happy every time.
