🖼看图演讲-90s–9

🧩 模板结构：4 段思路
1️⃣ Overview – what you see
2️⃣ Details – who / what / where
3️⃣ Action – what’s happening
4️⃣ Opinion – your guess or feeling

Template:

In this picture, I can see [main scene].

📄看题演讲-90s–10

任务类型：给一个观点题，比如 “Do you think people should work from home?”

🧩 模板结构
1️⃣ Introduction – 表明观点
2️⃣ Reason 1 + Example
3️⃣ Reason 2 + Example
4️⃣ Conclusion – 重申观点

Template:

I strongly believe that [your opinion].
First, [reason 1 + simple example].
Second, [reason 2 + example or effect].
Therefore, I think [repeat your stance clearly].

示例：

I strongly believe that people should be allowed to work from home.
First, it saves commuting (/kəˈmjuːtɪŋ/通勤) time and gives employees more flexibility (/ˌfleksəˈbɪləti/灵活性).
Second, it helps companies reduce (/rɪˈduːs/减少,降低) office costs (/kɔːst/费用).
Therefore (因此), I think remote (/rɪˈmoʊt/远程的) work is a great choice for both employees and companies.

示例：
新👉 (441字符)–85s左右
I strongly believe that people should be allowed to work from home.
First, it saves commuting time and gives employees more flexibility. When people stay at home, they can start work earlier because they do not need to sit on a bus or train for a long time.
Second, it helps companies reduce office costs. For example, they can use less space, less power.
Therefore, I think remote work is a great choice for both employees and companies.

🗓口语样本-3min–12

🧩 模板结构
1️⃣ Introduction – 明确主题
2️⃣ Point 1 – 理由 + 举例
3️⃣ Point 2 – 理由 + 举例
4️⃣ Point 3 – 理由 + 举例
5️⃣ Conclusion – 总结观点

Template:

Today I’d like to talk about [topic].
First of all, [reason 1 + example].
Secondly, [reason 2 + example].
Another point is that [reason 3 + example].
In conclusion, I think [restate topic + final thought].

示例：

Today I’d like to talk about the importance of teamwork.
First of all, teamwork helps people share ideas and learn from each other. For example, in my previous (/ˈpriːviəs/先前的) project, our group solved problems faster through discussion.
Secondly, teamwork improves efficiency (/ɪˈfɪʃ(ə)nsi/效率) because everyone has clear roles (/roʊlz/角色,任务) .
Another point is that it builds trust (/trʌst/信任) and motivation (/ˌmoʊtɪˈveɪʃ(ə)n/积极性).
In conclusion, I think teamwork is essential (/ɪˈsenʃ(ə)l/必不可少的) for both personal growth and company success (/səkˈses/成功 n.).

示例：
新👉 (816字符)–2min35s
Today I’d like to talk about the importance of teamwork.
First of all, teamwork helps people share ideas and learn from each other. For example, in my previous project, our group solved problems faster through discussion. When people talk and listen, they can find simple and clear ways to do the work.
Secondly, teamwork improves efficiency because everyone has clear roles. When each person knows what to do, the team can move forward in a smooth and easy way.
Another point is that it builds trust and motivation. People feel safe and strong when they work with others who support them, and this makes the whole team happier and more active.
In conclusion, I think teamwork is essential for both personal growth and company success, because it helps people work well, stay connected, and reach goals together.
