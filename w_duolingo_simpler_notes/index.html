写作

看图写句模板-60s

模板1:

This colorful (彩色的) and lively (活泼的, 有生气的) image shows (展示, 描绘) that … . The weather outside is sunny/cloudy/rainy.

👉 举例: This colorful and lively photo shows that a strong (强壮的) man is pushing two elderly (上了年纪的) people angrily. The weather outside is sunny.

模板2:

We can see in this interesting (有趣的) and special (特别的) picture that … . This lively image shows (展示) a great (极好的) situation.

👉 举例: We can see in this interesting and special picture that two playful (爱玩的, 调皮的) boys are playing basketball with each other. This lively image shows a great situation.

模板3:

It can be clearly (清楚地) seen in this great photo that … . I think (认为) he/she is feeling happy (高兴的) / unhappy (不高兴的) / satisfied (满意的).

👉 举例: It can be clearly seen in this great photo that a crying (正在哭的) boy is standing in front of a wall. I think he is being bullied(/ˈbʊlid/adj. 被欺负的；因害怕而屈服顺从的), because this colorful image shows a dangerous scene.

互动听力-总结题-75s

I was a student in a Political Science class, and I had a problem with finding more sources(/ˈsɔːrsɪz/n. 来源，出处；起源，根源；发源地（source 的复数）).

I asked my professor. Afterwards, she gave me some advice, which was that I should look at news articles and a book on the topic.

小作文5min

我保留了逻辑结构（3 段式），但换成简单自然、容易说和写的表达。

第一段（开头段）:

This topic about work schedules is quite common today. Some people think it is better to have flexible hours, while others prefer a fixed routine. In my opinion, being able to choose one’s own working time is better for most people.

第二段（主体段）:

First, flexible working hours help people keep a better balance between work and life. They can spend more time with their families or take care of personal things.
Also, flexible time makes workers feel more trusted and responsible, because they can plan their own work in a way that fits their life.

第三段（结尾段）:

In conclusion, I believe that choosing one’s own working hours is not only a benefit but also a need in modern life. This kind of freedom helps both employees and companies work more happily and efficiently.

互动写作

第1题-5min

任务类型：讨论观点、解释原因、表达立场
目标：逻辑完整、结构清晰、词汇自然、结尾总结

People have different opinions about working from home. In my view, I believe that remote work is better because it brings more flexibility and comfort.
First, people can save time by not commuting every day. For example, they can start work earlier or spend more time with family. Second, working at home helps employees feel more relaxed and productive. This improves work quality.
In conclusion, remote work is a modern and practical choice for most people.

第2题-3min

任务类型：简答、观点表达、快速理由说明
目标：句型多样、简洁自然、有衔接词但不过度

Template:

Structure (4 short parts)
1️⃣ I think [your opinion].
2️⃣ One reason is that [reason 1].
3️⃣ Another reason is that [reason 2 or example].
4️⃣ So, in my opinion, [repeat conclusion briefly].

示例：

I think students should have more time for sports.
One reason is that exercise helps them stay healthy.
Another reason is that it reduces stress from studying.
So, in my opinion, schools should give students more time to play sports every week.

🎙 口语

🗣互动口语-35s每题

任务类型：简答互动题（通常问意见、经历、偏好）
目标：自然、流畅、结构完整，2–3 句即可

模板1–表达观点类

Well, I think [topic] is quite important.
One reason is that [reason 1].
Also, it helps people [reason 2 or benefit].
So personally, I believe [restate your opinion briefly].

示例：

Well, I think reading books is quite important.
One reason is that it improves our imagination.
Also, it helps people learn how to express ideas clearly.
So personally, I believe reading is one of the best habits.

模板2–讲经历类

Yes, I have experienced that before.
I remember when I [short story or example].
It made me feel [emotion].
From that experience, I learned that [lesson].

示例：

Yes, I have experienced working under pressure.
I remember when I had to finish a project in one day.
It made me feel nervous but excited. From that experience, I
learned how to manage time efficiently.

模板3–比较选择类

I would prefer [option A] rather than [option B].
The main reason is that [reason 1].
Another reason is that [reason 2].
That’s why I’d choose [option A].

示例：

I would prefer working in a small company rather than a big one.
The main reason is that small teams are more flexible.
Another reason is that I can learn a wider range of skills.
That’s why I’d choose a smaller company.

🖼看图演讲-90s

目标：结构化描述 + 逻辑自然 + 语音连贯

🧩 模板结构：4 段思路
1️⃣ Overview – what you see
2️⃣ Details – who / what / where
3️⃣ Action – what’s happening
4️⃣ Opinion – your guess or feeling

Template:

In this picture, I can see [main scene].
There are [people/things] doing [activity]. It seems to be [place or time].
The person in the middle is [action or emotion]. Others are [more details].
I think this picture shows [theme or meaning], and it gives me a feeling of [emotion].

示例：

In this picture, I can see a young woman taking photos in a park.
There are some trees and flowers around her, and the weather looks sunny and warm.
The woman is holding a camera and seems very focused on what she’s shooting. A few people are walking in the background.
I think this picture shows someone enjoying nature and photography, and it gives me a feeling of peace and relaxation.

📄看题演讲-90s

任务类型：给一个观点题，比如 “Do you think people should work from home?”

🧩 模板结构
1️⃣ Introduction – 表明观点
2️⃣ Reason 1 + Example
3️⃣ Reason 2 + Example
4️⃣ Conclusion – 重申观点

Template:

I strongly believe that [your opinion].
First, [reason 1 + simple example].
Second, [reason 2 + example or effect].
Therefore, I think [repeat your stance clearly].

示例：

I strongly believe that people should be allowed to work from home.
First, it saves commuting time and gives employees more flexibility.
Second, it helps companies reduce office costs.
Therefore, I think remote work is a great choice for both employees and companies.

🎧听题演讲-90s

任务类型：听一段题目或情境后回答
重点：复述 + 自己观点 + 逻辑清晰

🧩 模板结构
1️⃣ Rephrase question
2️⃣ Give opinion
3️⃣ Give 2 reasons
4️⃣ Wrap up

Template:

The question is about [restate the question in your words].
In my opinion, [state your view].
One reason is that [reason 1 + short example].
Another reason is that [reason 2].
That’s why I believe [repeat conclusion briefly].

示例：

The question is about whether technology makes people’s lives easier.
In my opinion, yes, it does.
One reason is that technology saves a lot of time in daily tasks.
Another reason is that it helps us communicate instantly.
That’s why I believe technology brings more convenience than problems.

🗓口语样本-3min

🧩 模板结构
1️⃣ Introduction – 明确主题
2️⃣ Point 1 – 理由 + 举例
3️⃣ Point 2 – 理由 + 举例
4️⃣ Point 3 – 理由 + 举例
5️⃣ Conclusion – 总结观点

Template:

Today I’d like to talk about [topic].
First of all, [reason 1 + example].
Secondly, [reason 2 + example].
Another point is that [reason 3 + example].
In conclusion, I think [restate topic + final thought].

示例：

Today I’d like to talk about the importance of teamwork.
First of all, teamwork helps people share ideas and learn from each other. For example, in my previous project, our group solved problems faster through discussion.
Secondly, teamwork improves efficiency because everyone has clear roles.
Another point is that it builds trust and motivation.
In conclusion, I think teamwork is essential for both personal growth and company success.
